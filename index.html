Individuals Experiencing Poverty

02

~329,000 children in US foster care. 78% experience basic needs insecurity. We provide mentorship, education, and care programs.

Widows Seeking Stability
Widows lose up to 75% of their support systems. ~700,000 women are widowed each year. We provide vocational training and community belonging.

Immigrants face a 20.9% poverty rate vs 9.9% for non-immigrants. We create networks of opportunity, inclusion, and career pathways.

Our Story

About
Tessera

Tessera Compassion Foundation was founded in 2025 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit—but its story began long before its official establishment.

What started as a simple act of compassion—a day of rest for elderly individuals tirelessly working through poverty in Korea—quickly grew into a movement. By sharing their stories, we ignited a wave of empathy that spread across social media, inspiring others to take action.

What began as storytelling evolved into a platform connecting digital awareness with real-world solutions. Today, Tessera turns moments of kindness into measurable, lasting impact—transforming empathy into empowerment and awareness into meaningful change.

Our Foundation
Mission
Uplift the Marginalized
Our mission is to uplift marginalized populations, including the elderly, orphans, widows, individuals experiencing poverty, and immigrant communities.
Purpose
Provide the Building Blocks
Our purpose is to provide the building blocks—tessera—necessary to those who need it most. Through grassroots initiatives, digital storytelling, and strategic partnerships, we create pathways to self-sufficiency.
Vision
A World Where Everyone Thrives
Our vision is a world where every marginalized person, regardless of circumstance, is seen, supported, and empowered to thrive.

About the Founder
Aiden Kim
Licensed Pharmacist · Founder & President, TCF

Aiden Kim's journey began not in a boardroom, but on the street. A licensed pharmacist, he discovered his calling to serve underprivileged communities during his student years in Stockton, California.

Guided by empathy and purpose, he combined his compassion for others with the power of social media to address social challenges head-on. What began as sharing stories of resilience among elderly Koreans living in poverty soon evolved into a global movement proving that compassion is contagious.

Through his storytelling, Aiden witnessed how digital platforms could unite people across borders—raising funds, spreading hope, and transforming awareness into tangible impact. These experiences inspired him to found Tessera Compassion Foundation, dedicated to turning moments of empathy into sustainable change.
